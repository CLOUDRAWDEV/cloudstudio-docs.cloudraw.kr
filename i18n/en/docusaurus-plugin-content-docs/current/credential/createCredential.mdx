> -Â Â  9-9. Search for and select the users, groups, or service principals.
> -Â Â  9-10. Click `Select` to add the users, groups, or service principals to the list of members.
> -Â Â  9-11. To assign the selected role to one or more managed identities, select `Managed Identity`.
> -Â Â  9-12. Click `Select managed identities`.

> -Â Â  9-14. Search for and select the managed identities.
> -Â Â  9-15. Click `Select` to add the managed identities to the list of members, then click `Next`.
> -Â Â  9-16. Click `Review + assign` to assign the role. The role will be assigned to the security principal in the selected scope after a few minutes.
> -Â Â  9-17. If a description for the role assignment is not displayed, click `Edit columns` to add the description column.
